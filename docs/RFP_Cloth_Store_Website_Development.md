## 1. Introduction & Background

[Your Cloth Store Name] is a well-established cloth retailer based in [City, Maharashtra, India], specializing in quality apparel for the entire family. Our product range includes:

- **Men’s Wear**: Formal shirts, trousers, casuals, ethnic wear, and accessories
- **Women’s Wear**: Sarees, kurtis, salwar suits, western wear, fusion, and Indo-western
- **Kids’ Wear**: Clothing for boys and girls across age groups
- **Fabrics & Accessories**: Premium fabrics, dupattas, stoles, and fashion accessories

Currently, we serve customers primarily through our physical retail outlet(s) and maintain an active presence on social media platforms. To expand our customer base across India, improve accessibility, increase sales, and deliver a superior shopping experience, we have decided to develop a full-fledged, professional e-commerce website.

This Request for Proposal (RFP) invites qualified web development agencies, freelancers, or software companies to submit proposals for the design, development, deployment, and initial support of our online store.

## 2. Project Objectives

The key objectives of this website development project are:

- Build a visually appealing, brand-consistent, and conversion-optimized e-commerce platform.
- Enable customers to effortlessly discover, compare, and purchase products online.
- Provide a seamless, secure, and convenient checkout experience with popular Indian payment methods.
- Deliver a powerful yet easy-to-use admin dashboard for managing products, inventory, orders, and customers.
- Ensure the website is fully responsive, fast-loading, SEO-friendly, and future-scalable.
- Integrate modern marketing and support tools such as WhatsApp, analytics, and promotional features.
- Establish a strong digital foundation that supports long-term business growth and omnichannel retail.

## 3. Scope of Work

The selected vendor will be responsible for the complete lifecycle — from discovery and design to development, testing, deployment, training, and handover.

### 3.1 Design & User Experience (UI/UX)
- Custom, modern UI/UX design aligned with our brand identity (colors, typography, logo, visual language).
- High-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes (Figma or Adobe XD).
- Intuitive information architecture and navigation (mega menu, category structure, breadcrumbs).
- Advanced product discovery: Search with autocomplete, filters (price, size, color, fabric, occasion, brand, discount), sorting, and faceted navigation.
- Rich Product Detail Pages (PDP) featuring multiple high-quality images with zoom, 360° view (optional), variant selection (size/color), size charts, related products, and customer reviews/ratings.
- Streamlined shopping cart, wishlist, “Buy Now”, and “Add to Cart” flows.
- Mobile-first, responsive design ensuring excellent experience across devices.
- Accessibility considerations (WCAG 2.1 AA level where practical).

### 3.2 Core E-commerce Features
- Complete product catalog management (categories, sub-categories, collections, tags).
- Variant management (size, color, material) with separate SKU, price, and stock for each variant.
- Inventory management with real-time stock tracking and low-stock notifications.
- Pricing engine supporting regular price, sale price, and special offers.
- Full order lifecycle management (place → confirm → process → ship → deliver/cancel) with status updates and customer notifications.
- Customer accounts with registration/login (email + password, phone OTP), profile management, saved addresses, and order history.
- Discount & promotion engine: Percentage discounts, flat discounts, coupon codes, minimum cart value rules, and seasonal campaigns.
- Abandoned cart recovery via automated email sequences.
- Invoice generation (GST-compliant PDF invoices).

### 3.3 Payments, Shipping & Compliance (India-Specific)
- Secure integration with leading Indian payment gateways — **Razorpay** (primary recommendation), with support for UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular wallets. Additional gateways (PayU/PhonePe) as backup.
- Cash on Delivery (COD) option with configurable pincode restrictions if needed.
- Automatic GST calculation and tax-compliant invoicing.
- Shipping rate configuration (flat, weight-based, or pincode-based) or real-time integration with logistics partners such as **Shiprocket**, Delhivery, or Ecom Express (including tracking via AWB).
- Order tracking page for customers.

### 3.4 Admin Dashboard & Operations
- Modern, intuitive admin panel with role-based access control (Super Admin, Manager, Inventory Staff, etc.).
- Real-time dashboard showing key metrics: total revenue, orders, average order value, top-selling products, conversion rates (visual charts).
- Comprehensive reporting module (daily/weekly/monthly sales reports, bestsellers, customer segmentation).
- Bulk product import/export via CSV/Excel.
- Homepage and promotional content management (hero banners, category banners, featured collections, flash sale sections).
- CMS for static pages (About Us, Contact, Shipping & Returns Policy, Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions) and optional blog/news section.
- Customer management and support ticket/notes system.

### 3.5 Technical Requirements
Vendors should propose the most suitable modern technology stack. We are open to well-reasoned recommendations but have the following preferences:

**Preferred / Recommended Stack:**
- **Frontend**: Next.js (React) or React.js for high performance and excellent SEO
- **Backend**: Laravel (PHP) or Node.js (NestJS/Express)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL or MySQL
- **Headless Commerce** (optional but welcome): Medusa.js or similar for future scalability

Alternative acceptable solutions:
- WooCommerce on WordPress (if budget-optimized and customization needs are moderate)
- Other robust frameworks with clean, maintainable code

**Mandatory Non-Functional Requirements:**
- Excellent Core Web Vitals and page load performance (< 3 seconds on average 4G connection)
- Image optimization (WebP, AVIF, lazy loading, responsive images)
- CDN integration (Cloudflare recommended)
- Strong security practices (HTTPS, secure auth, protection against OWASP Top 10, regular security updates)
- SEO best practices: Proper meta tags, XML sitemap, structured data (Product + Offer schema), clean URLs, canonical tags
- Google Analytics 4 + Google Tag Manager + Meta Pixel integration
- WhatsApp Business API integration for order notifications, shipping updates, and live chat/support button
- Email & SMS notification system (using reliable providers such as Brevo, MSG91, or Twilio)
- API-first architecture to allow future mobile app development
- Automated daily backups and monitoring setup

### 3.6 Hosting, Domain & Deployment
- Recommendation and one-time setup of reliable, scalable hosting environment (AWS, Vercel + Railway/Render, Cloudways, or a good Indian provider).
- Domain configuration and SSL certificate installation (we will provide domain or assist in purchase).
- Production deployment, staging environment for testing, and CI/CD pipeline setup where applicable.
- Post-launch monitoring and basic performance optimization.

### 3.7 Training, Documentation & Handover
- Hands-on training sessions for the internal team (minimum 2–3 sessions, recorded for future reference).
- Comprehensive user manual (Admin & Operations) in PDF/video format.
- Technical documentation including architecture overview, API documentation (if custom), and deployment guide.
- Full source code access via Git repository (or complete handover) upon final payment.
- 3 months of complimentary post-launch support (bug fixes and minor adjustments).
